When user open the location-based quiz app, please make sure that you have already turn on

your GPS. Once the app laucnh, it will track your current location, which is shown in a blue

tab, as well as surronding by a red circle.

If you cannot see it, please click "Click Me to Track Location" buttom

Meanwhile, you will see four pink pins, which present the question marker. That means if you get

close to that specific location, a question will be pop-up for you to answer.

E.g. Chadwick Building, and when you get to the location, a list of questions will be provided

to tell you more about UCL's culture, history and environment.

And the red pin indicates the Warrent Street Station, so that if you are away from UCL, you will have

an idea that how to get to UCL.

Also, you can get the distance of Warrent Street Station from your current location by clicking

"Click me to Track Location" Buttom.

Once the user get into 10 meters of specific locaiton in radius, such as Chadwick Building or Science Library,

A pop up message will be shown and tell user to answer the specific question by clicking side-menu. And specific

question number is stated in the pop-up message as well.

After choosing the question by clicking side0menu, you will be lead to a new page, where contain 1 question and 4

possible answer. And now you are able to choose on of the best answer that you think. The correct answer will be showen

after you click "submit" buttom. And finally, you may click "Return to Home Page", to track the next question markers
